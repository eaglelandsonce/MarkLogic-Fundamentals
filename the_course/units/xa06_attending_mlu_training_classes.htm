Attending MLU Training Classes

MarkLogic University offers live online classes, as well as self-paced courses, taught by expert MarkLogic instructors.

Learning tracks can be accessed at www.marklogic.com/training.

For access to all of the courses, there is a Courses link (in the menu bar).

Dates and times of the upcoming live classes can be viewed at mlu.marklogic.com/registration.

Self-paced courses can be accessed via mlu.marklogic.com/selfpaced.
