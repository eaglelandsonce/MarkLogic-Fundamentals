Exercise: Access Conference Recordings

In this walkthrough exercise, you check the dates and location of the next MarkLogic World, and then view conference session recordings from the previous MarkLogic World.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to world.marklogic.com, to get the dates and location for the next MarkLogic World.
  2. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/resources/?fwp_marklogic_specific=marklogic-world.
  3. Scroll down to skim through the recordings of presentations from the most recent MarkLogic World.
  4. Select one of the videos (e.g. MarkLogic World 2019 Keynote with CEO Gary Bloom), to access the recording of the session:

  5. Watch the session.
  6. Continue perusing the conference session recordings, as desired.
