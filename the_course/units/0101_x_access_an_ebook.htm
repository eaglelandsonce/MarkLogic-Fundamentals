Exercise: Access an eBook

In this walkthrough exercise, you access an Enterprise NoSQL eBook, and the Data Hub Guide for Architects eBook.

 

  1. On the desktop of your virtual machine, In the mls-fundamentals/unit01 folder, double-click the Enterprise_NoSQL_for_Dummies.pdf file, to open the Enterprise NoSQL for Dummies book:


  2. Skim through the book, noting the various enterprise NoSQL topics, such as schema-agnostic, ACID, commodity hardware, scalability, high availability, replication, and enterprise-class security.
  3. Continue perusing the book, as desired.
  4. In a browser window, navigate to www.marklogic.com/resources/data-hub-guide-for-architects.
  5. Skim the book description.
  6. If desired, fill out the form to download the eBook:



    Note: If you would prefer to not fill out the form, you can access the book in the mls-fundamentals/unit01 folder.
  7. In the book, scroll down to the Executive Summary:


  8. Skim the text.
  9. Continue perusing the book, as desired.
  10. If desired, access the MarkLogic Data Hub documentation at docs.marklogic.com/datahub.
