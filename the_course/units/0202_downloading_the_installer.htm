Downloading the Installer

The MarkLogic installer can be downloaded from developer.marklogic.com/downloads.

The Downloads page contains the installation files for the supported operating systems.

Licensing

MarkLogic offers various license and pricing options, including Data Hub Service pricing, cloud platform pricing, enterprise subscription licensing, and developer licensing (free). Details for each can be found on the pricing page at www.marklogic.com/pricing.

The developer license is for developers, and comes with all of the MarkLogic features. It can be clustered across multiple computers and has a 1TB size limitation. It is for non-production use only, and has a six month time limit. New licenses may be requested on expiration. Developer license detail is at developer.marklogic.com/free-developer.
