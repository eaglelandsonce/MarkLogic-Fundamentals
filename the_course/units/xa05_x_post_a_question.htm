Exercise: Post a Question

In this walkthrough exercise, you see how you could post a question on Stack Overflow.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to stackoverflow.com.
  2. In the search field, enter MarkLogic.
  3. Notice that you are viewing questions and answers for MarkLogic.
  4. Click the Newest button, to see the most recent posts.
  5. In the upper-right, click the Ask Question button.
  6. See that you are prompted to log in or sign up.
  7. If you already have a log in, go ahead and log in. If not, select the sign up link, to register.
  8. If desired, post a question.
