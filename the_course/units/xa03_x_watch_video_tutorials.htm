Exercise: Watch Video Tutorials

In this walkthrough exercise, you access a video tutorial on a MarkLogic feature.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to mlu.marklogic.com/ondemand.
  2. Scroll down to view the concepts listed in the lower-left navigation.
  3. Click the More link, to see more of the concepts listed.
  4. Click a concept that looks interesting to you (e.g. Geospatial).
  5. Click the name of a video that looks interesting to you (e.g. Using Geospatial Data in MarkLogic).
  6. Click the Start button.
  7. Wait for the video to start downloading.
  8. Watch the video.
  9. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/resources/?fwp_resource_types=webinars.
  10. Scroll down through the list of webinars, noting ones of interest to you.
  11. If desired, click the name of one of the recordings (e.g. Lower ETL Costs and Improve Data Agility with a MarkLogic Data Hub), fill out the form to access the recording, and watch the webinar.
  12. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/events/?fwp_event_types=online to view upcoming webinars.
  13. Scroll down through the list of events, noting upcoming webinars of interest to you.
  14. If desired, register to watch an upcoming webinar.
