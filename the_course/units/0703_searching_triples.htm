Searching Triples

Triples are most commonly used to express relationships between entities. And, by querying across triples, interesting discoveries can be made.

SPARQL (SPARQL Protocol and RDF Query Language) is the standard query language for searching triples.

﻿SELECT ?o ?o2
WHERE {<http://marklogic.com/mlu/star-wars/resource/Han_Solo> <http://marklogic.com/mlu/star-wars/ontology/playedBy> ?o .
?o <http://marklogic.com/mlu/ontology/birthDate> ?o2}

SPARQL also includes techniques for making query results human-readable (e.g. Harrison Ford instead of ﻿<http://marklogic.com/mlu/resource/Harrison_Ford>).

SPARQL is a W3C Recommendation, fully supported in MarkLogic.
